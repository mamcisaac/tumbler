How to play

  • Goal: sort the colours so every tumbler holds just one colour (one tumbler may be left empty).
  • Pour: tap a tumbler to pick it up, then tap another to pour its top colour across. You can only pour onto a matching colour, or into a tumbler with room.
  • Rotate: the Rotate button turns the whole rack over — the bottom of every tumbler becomes the top and the beads fall. It's the only way to reach colours buried at the bottom.
  • Every pour and every rotate costs one move. Solve in as few moves as you can.
  • Unlimited undo & restart. Unlike the rest of the arcade, you can replay the daily as often as you like — your best score is the one that counts, so keep trimming moves.
